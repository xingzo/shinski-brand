## DJ Shinski Hits 2 Million YouTube Subscribers, Cementing Status as One of Africa's Most-Followed DJs

*The Kenyan-born Afrobeat and Amapiano curator reaches the milestone with a catalog spanning African, R&B, soul, and hip hop nonstop mixes*

---

**NAIROBI, Kenya — March 2026** — DJ Shinski, the Kenyan-born DJ and music curator, has surpassed 2 million subscribers on YouTube, making him one of the most-followed African DJs on the platform. The milestone caps years of consistent output across Afrobeat, Amapiano, old school R&B, soul, hip hop, and reggae — genres that have earned him a loyal, Pan-African audience stretching from Nairobi to Lagos to Johannesburg.

### A Catalog That Crosses Borders and Genres

DJ Shinski's rise has been built on a prolific library of nonstop video mixes and mixtapes that blend African sounds with global genres. His Afrobeat mixes have become a staple for listeners across East and West Africa, while his Amapiano mixes tap into one of the continent's fastest-growing musical movements. At the same time, his old school R&B and soul collections have cultivated a dedicated following among listeners who want curated journeys through 80s and 90s classics.

This genre range is central to what makes the 2 million subscriber milestone significant: it was not built on a single sound, but on an audience that trusts DJ Shinski to curate across the full spectrum of African and Black music.

DJ Shinski's subscriber base reflects the breadth of his music. His largest audiences are in Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, South Africa, Uganda, and Ghana — the core markets of the African music industry.
